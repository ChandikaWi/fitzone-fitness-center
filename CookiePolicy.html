Cookie Policy

Learn how FitZone Fitness Center uses cookies to enhance your website experience.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve your experience by remembering your preferences and tracking usage patterns. See our Privacy Policy for more details on data handling.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as maintaining your session during login or registration.
  • Analytics Cookies: Collect anonymous data on how you use our website to help us improve its performance and content.
  • Preference Cookies: Store your settings, such as light or dark theme preferences, to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant promotions, but only with your consent.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but this may affect website functionality. For instructions, consult your browser’s help resources.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g., Google Analytics) that set cookies to analyze website traffic. These cookies are governed by the respective third-party privacy policies.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
